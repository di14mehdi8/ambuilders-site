04 — About

Niche by design.
Nashville by address.

AM Builders is a niche commercial contractor. We do renovations and smaller ground-up work — typically under $2 million — in restaurant and retail, industrial, pre-engineered metal buildings, and site work. We also take on luxury residential renovation projects where the level of detail demands commercial discipline.

Founded in 2022 and licensed in Tennessee (GC #77974), we are built for a city in motion. Nashville is one of the fastest-growing markets in the country, and our size lets us move with it — senior attention on every job, crews who show up, and a schedule you can plan a business around.
